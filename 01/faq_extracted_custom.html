About

What is MyFastRx?

MyFastRx is a telehealth platform connecting you to U.S.-licensed physicians for online consultations and prescription medication delivered to your home.

How does the process work?

Pick your treatment, answer a brief set of medical questions, and a doctor will review within ~24 hours. Approved prescriptions ship free and discreetly.

How much does it cost?

Pricing is flat and transparent. Semaglutide is $169/mo and Tirzepatide is $249/mo — consultation, medication, and shipping included.

Do you accept insurance?

MyFastRx does not require insurance. Our prices are designed to be more affordable than most pharmacies even without coverage.

Is my information secure?

Yes. All consultations and records are protected under HIPAA standards.
