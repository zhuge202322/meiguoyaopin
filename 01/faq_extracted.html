About

What is MyFastRx?

At MyFastRx, we serve as your comprehensive health and wellness partner for various conditions. Through our platform, MyFastRx facilitates virtual medical consultations and coordinates cost-effective prescription treatments, all within the comfort of your home—the place where you feel most at ease. Our expertise extends beyond conditions that may cause discomfort, encompassing acute, subacute, and chronic disease care. Every one of our physicians, based in the US, conducts a thorough assessment of each patient and prescribes medication therapy when suitable. Our mission centers on delivering accessible, affordable, and top-notch healthcare solutions to individuals and communities spanning multiple states. Continually expanding our offerings, MyFastRx provides diverse care options, irrespective of insurance coverage, at a fraction of our competitors’ rates. Explore our services firsthand, complete with complimentary 2-day shipping and an unmatched convenient online experience.

What is the MyFastRx process?

Choose the therapy required, click on ‘Start My Visit,’ and respond to a brief set of medical questions. Our team of US-based doctors will thoroughly review your provided information and prescribe medications as necessary. Expect to receive feedback from one of our doctors within approximately 24 hours. In certain States, a video consultation or live chat will be necessary. If so, one of our doctors will reach out to you to conduct and finalize your consultation using the required method.

How much does MyFastRx cost?

You may qualify for a consultation with your order. Should medication be suitable, you can acquire it via the MyFastRx platform at significantly reduced prices compared to other websites, often even more cost-effective than your neighborhood pharmacy.

Other

Can I use my health insurance?

We do not accept insurance. We believe an individual’s access to quality care should not depend on a third party’s opinion. MyFastRx has removed the barriers traditional insurers can cause and replaced them with transparency and convenience. Our prices for your doctor consultation and medication are industry-leading and oftentimes cheaper than using insurance.

Is it legal to prescribe medications online?

Yes. Each doctor you interact with is licensed to prescribe medicine and treat patients in the state you live in. This allows you to get the medications you need, on your schedule, hassle-free.

Can I use my local pharmacy?

Yes. You can choose to use your local pharmacy and our doctors can send your prescription to them. However, the cheapest pricing and most convenient option will be through MyFastRx’s network of affiliated pharmacies. We cannot guarantee drug pricing at your local pharmacy.

Do I need to sign for my order?

No. Your medication will ship via Rush No-Contact delivery, a signature is not required.

How do I change my credit card?

You can change your method of payment at any time. Login to your MyFastRx account and follow the prompts.

What if I have a problem with my order or my account?

Click on the “Contact us” section of this site and create a ticket. Our team will then review and get back to you to resolve your inquiry.

How long will it take to get my medication?

Once you complete the online visit, you’ll hear back from one of our US-based doctors in 24 hours (some States will require a live chat or video consultation). Orders will then be sent to the dispensing pharmacy and shipped.

How can I change my shipping address?

Login to your MyFastRx account and update your shipping address at any time. Once your medication is shipped and delivered, you can change your address for all future shipments.

Returns

How do I reorder or cancel?

Login to your MyFastRx account and reorder or cancel at any time. You will be contacted before your medication is to be refilled and billed to your credit card. Your medication will then be shipped.

Can I return my medications once I receive them?

Federal and State law do not allow medication returns. Therefore, all sales are final. If you have a specific concern, please create a ticket in the “Contact us” section and we’ll get back with you.
