      No office visit is required. All consultations are done securely online with a licensed U.S. physician. Once approved, your medication is shipped directly to you – fast and discreet.

    • Who is eligible for GLP-1 treatment?

      GLP-1 medications like Semaglutide and Tirzepatide are typically prescribed for adults with a BMI of 27 or higher who want to lose weight safely and effectively. During your online visit, a doctor will review your medical history to confirm eligibility.

    • Are MyFastRx doctors licensed and legitimate?

      Yes. All MyFastRx providers are U.S.-licensed physicians experienced in weight management and telemedicine. Your consultation, prescription, and ongoing support are fully compliant with state medical and pharmacy regulations.

    • How long does it take to start?

      Most patients complete the online visit in 12-14 minutes. A doctor typically reviews your case within 24 hours, and your medication is shipped shortly after approval.

    • Is shipping really free and discreet?

      Yes. All orders include free, discreet shipping directly to your home. Packages arrive in plain, unmarked boxes with no obvious medical labeling for your privacy.

    • What if I need a dosage adjustment?

      Your doctor will guide dosage adjustments as needed – always included in your flat monthly price. There are no extra fees for dose changes or follow-ups.

    • Can I cancel anytime?

      Yes. You can pause or cancel your treatment anytime before your next monthly refill. There are no cancellation or membership fees – you’re always in control.

    • Are GLP-1 medications safe?

      Yes, when prescribed and monitored by a doctor. Semaglutide and Tirzepatide are clinically proven, These medications are used to help manage weight and improve metabolic health. Your MyFastRx provider ensures the dosage and plan are safe for your unique health profile.

    • What Are GLP-1 medications?

      GLP-1 medications are a class of prescription drugs that not only improve blood sugar control but may also lead to weight loss. This class of drugs is commonly called glucagon-like peptide 1 (GLP-1) agonists. Weight loss can vary, depending on which GLP-1 drug you use and the dosage.

    • How to take GLP-1 medication?

      It is important to follow the prescribed doses and directions from your provider accurately in order to minimize risk and/or severity of potential side effects. If you have any questions about how to take your medication once you receive it, you should reach out to your doctor in the MyFastRx portal before taking. Taking doses other than what is prescribed may have unintended and potentially dangerous side effects.

    • What should I do if I miss a dosage?

      If you miss a dosage on your scheduled day, you should reach out to your doctor in the MyFastRx portal before continuing your dosage. You may need to lessen your next dose in order to minimize risk or severity of side effects.

    • How should I store GLP-1 medications?

      These medications should be stored in the refrigerator between doses. They are sensitive to temperature and could degrade if not stored properly.

    • When should I take my dose of GLP-1 medication?

      You can take GLP-1 medications at any time during the day, regardless of meals. For weekly dosages, you should take your dose on the same day each week

    • Can I take a dosage that I’ve already been on if I’m switching to MyFastRx from another doctor?

      During your intake process, you should discuss your previous prescription and dosage with the MyFastRx provider. If medically appropriate, the provider may coordinate your dosage according to your previous regimen
